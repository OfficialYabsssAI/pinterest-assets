Privacy Policy — Yabsss Studio

Last updated: July 2026

Yabsss Studio ("we," "us") operates a Pinterest application used solely to publish and manage pins on our own Pinterest business account, for our own content and products.

What we access

Our application connects to a single Pinterest business account that we own and operate. Through Pinterest's API, it can:

  • Create and schedule pins on our own boards
  • Read our own boards and pins (to organize and track what has been posted)

We do not access, request, or store data belonging to any other Pinterest user or account. This application is not distributed to or used by third parties.

What we don't do

  • We do not collect personal data from visitors or from other Pinterest users
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties
  • We do not use this integration for advertising, tracking, or profiling

Data storage

Authentication tokens for our own Pinterest account are stored securely and used only to authorize the actions described above. Content we publish (pin images, titles, descriptions) is our own original material.
